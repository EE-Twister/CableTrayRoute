Sustainability Footprint

Calculate embodied CO₂e (Scope 3, A1–A3 cradle-to-gate) from project BOM and operating CO₂e (Scope 2) from lifetime conductor losses. Suitable for LEED, BREEAM, and client sustainability appendices.

Method & Standards

Embodied CO₂e (Scope 3): Each BOM line item's quantity is multiplied by a unit embodied factor (kg CO₂e per metre or per unit). Factors are sourced from published EPD averages per IEC/EN 15804 (A1–A3, cradle-to-gate). Item-level co2eKgPerUnit overrides take precedence when EPD data is available for a specific product.

Operating CO₂e (Scope 2): Annual conductor losses (kW) × 8 760 h/yr × grid emission factor (kg CO₂e/kWh) × project life (years). Grid factors are national average generation-mix values from IEA and EPA eGRID.

Scope boundaries: This tool covers Scope 2 (purchased electricity losses) and Scope 3 Category 1 (purchased goods and materials — embodied carbon). Scope 1 (on-site diesel for generators) and transport emissions are excluded from the default calculation.

Disclaimer: Factors are representative industry averages for screening-level design. Final sustainability reports and LEED/BREEAM submittals must be verified against manufacturer-specific EPDs and the project-specific grid emission factor.

Sustainability Footprint Help

Select a grid region and project life, then click Calculate Footprint. The tool reads your project's cable schedule, raceway schedule, and equipment list to assemble a BOM and roll up embodied CO₂e.

Grid region: Sets the national average grid emission factor (kg CO₂e per kWh). Use "Custom" to enter a project-specific factor (e.g. from a PPA or renewable certificate).

Operating losses: Select "From IEC 60287 study results" to pull the total conductor losses calculated in the Cable Ampacity study, or enter a manual value. Losses are capitalised over the project life using the selected grid factor.

Skipped items: BOM entries whose size, material, or category cannot be matched to the library appear in the Skipped Items panel. Add a co2eKgPerUnit field to the item or use a supported size/material to include it.

Alternative comparison: Enable the comparison toggle and paste a BOM JSON array for the alternative design. The tool shows the embodied delta (positive = alternative is heavier, negative = lighter).

Use Export CSV to download the full line-item breakdown for inclusion in sustainability reports.
