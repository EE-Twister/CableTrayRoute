# Cable Fill Rules

Cable trays and conduits are evaluated by comparing the total cross-sectional area of installed cables against the available area.

Fill percentage is computed as:

\[ F = 100 \times \frac{\sum A_{\text{cable}}}{A_{\text{space}}} \]

Where \(A_{\text{cable}}\) is the area of each cable and \(A_{\text{space}}\) is the usable area of the tray or conduit. A generic 40% conduit or 50% tray fraction is only a preliminary screen. Final limits depend on raceway type, conductor count, cable size/type, voltage, tray construction, and mixed-cable arrangements. Use the rule-specific calculator and applicable NEC tables for the final check.
