Raceway Schedule Help

Use these tables to manage raceway information. Each section offers a Help button with usage tips and example templates for import/export.

Ductbank Schedule Help

Add ductbanks and expand rows to include conduits. Export to XLSX to create a workbook with Ductbanks and Conduits sheets. Templates: Ductbanks CSV, Conduits CSV.

Tray Schedule Help

List tray segments with start/end coordinates and dimensions. Use Import/Export to work with Excel files. Template: tray_schedule.csv.

Conduit Schedule Help

Record stand-alone conduits with start/end coordinates, type, trade size, and capacity. Template: conduit_schedule.csv.
