IEC 60909-0:2016

Equivalent Voltage Source Method

Select maximum (c_max) for equipment rating checks or minimum (c_min) for protection coordination settings.

Study Parameters

Voltage Factor (c) c_max gives the highest fault current for checking equipment interrupt ratings. c_min gives the lowest fault current for verifying protection device sensitivity. Applies only to LV buses (≤ 1 kV). MV/HV buses always use c_max = 1.10. Per IEC 60909-0:2016 Table 1.
Thermal Equivalent Current (Ith) Duration of the short circuit in seconds. Used to calculate Ith. Typical values: 0.1 s (fast fuse), 0.5 s (circuit breaker), 1.0 s (relay), 3.0 s (bus bar rating). IEC 60909 is defined for 50 Hz systems. 60 Hz is supported for international projects.
