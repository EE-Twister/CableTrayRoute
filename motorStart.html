Motor Starting

Simulate voltage sag from large motor inrush.

Supported Starter Types

Set starter_type on each motor component in the One-Line Diagram to model different starting methods.

starter_type Inrush Model Key Parameters
dol (default) Full locked-rotor current (6× FLC typical) inrushMultiple
vfd Current limited to vfd_current_limit_pu × FLC (default 1.1×) vfd_current_limit_pu, ramp_time_s
soft_starter Voltage ramps from initial_voltage_pu to 1.0; inrush ∝ V² initial_voltage_pu (0.3), ramp_time_s (10)
wye_delta Inrush = Ilr ÷ 3 during wye phase; full inrush after switch wye_delta_switch_time_s (5)
autotransformer Inrush × tap²; typical tap 0.65 autotransformer_tap (0.65)
