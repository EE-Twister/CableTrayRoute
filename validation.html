Methodology & Disclaimer

CableTrayRoute calculations implement published engineering standards and are intended for screening-level and detailed design analysis. All outputs should be reviewed by a licensed Professional Engineer before use in construction documents or safety-critical decisions.

  • Each study page shows a Calculation Basis panel listing the governing standard, key formulas, and known limitations.
  • Benchmark cases on this page reproduce published example problems with tolerance-band assertions verified by automated tests.
  • Where gaps exist between the implemented method and the full standard (e.g., near-to-generator correction factors), these are documented in the relevant study's Calculation Basis panel and in the limitations table below.
  • This tool is a browser-native engineering aid, not a replacement for dedicated power-system analysis platforms such as ETAP, EasyPower, or SKM for projects requiring full dynamic simulation, three-phase unbalanced analysis, or regulatory filing.
