Cathodic Protection (CP) Sizing

Method, Formulas, and Standards Mapping

This page provides preliminary impressed-current or galvanic CP sizing for buried assets. Inputs and formulas are aligned with common practice from NACE SP0169 / AMPP SP21424, ISO 15589-1, and DNV-RP-B401 style current-demand and anode-consumption methods.

I_req = A_exposed × i_d
W_req = (I_req × t_design_hours) / (Q_anode × U × F_design)
Life_years = (W_installed × Q_anode × U × F_design) / (I_req × 8760)
