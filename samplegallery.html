About This Gallery

Each sample is a complete, ready-to-explore project. Click Open Sample to load it as your current project and follow the guided checklist to tour the relevant pages. Use Download JSON to save a copy for offline reference.

Samples cover the most common electrical design workflows: cable routing, tray fill, arc flash, TCC coordination, ground grid, heat trace, underground ductbank, and load flow.

    Open Sample loads the sample into your current project workspace. Your existing project data will be replaced — save first if needed.
