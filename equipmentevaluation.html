Equipment Evaluation

About This Study

Checks every equipment item on the one-line diagram against available fault current from the short-circuit and arc-flash studies. Run the Short Circuit and Arc Flash studies first to get meaningful results.

AIC Check
Interrupting rating of breakers and fuses vs. 3-phase bolted fault current (NEC 110.9).
Withstand Check
Short-time withstand rating of breakers and switchgear vs. fault current × clearing time (I²t rule).
Cable Thermal Duty
Conductor cross-section vs. minimum required by I²t formula per NEC 110.10 / IEC 60364-5-54.
SCCR
Short-Circuit Current Rating for motor-control equipment vs. available fault current (NEC 409.22).

Equipment Evaluation Help

This study reads protective device ratings from the one-line diagram and compares them against fault current results from the Short Circuit and Arc Flash studies.

Prerequisites: Run the Short Circuit study (and optionally the Arc Flash study for clearing times) before evaluating equipment.

Adding Ratings: Open the One-Line editor, select a component, and enter Interrupt Rating (kA), Withstand Rating (kA), and Withstand Cycles in the component properties panel. For cable segments, enter the conductor size and insulation type.

SCCR: For MCC components, enter the SCCR value in the component properties panel.
