Cross-Study Design Coach

About This Tool

The Design Coach scans all study results in your project and generates specific, actionable recommendations ranked by severity. Run it after completing your electrical studies to identify the highest-priority design changes.

Safety
Arc flash exposure exceeding PPE Category 4, ground grid touch/step voltage violations — require immediate engineering action.
Compliance
Voltage drop exceeding NEC limits, tray fill over 40%, harmonic THD above IEEE 519 limits, short-circuit warnings, bus voltages outside ANSI C84.1 Range A, GPR exceedance.
Efficiency
Cables approaching voltage drop limits — monitor during final load calculations.
Missing Data
Studies with incomplete input data that may produce unreliable results.

Design Coach Help

The Design Coach reads cable, raceway, and study data from your current project and cross-checks all results for violations and improvement opportunities.

Prerequisites: Complete at least some of the electrical studies (load flow, short circuit, arc flash, voltage drop, harmonics, ground grid) to get meaningful recommendations. The coach will note any studies with missing data.

Accept / Dismiss: Use Accept to document an engineering basis for allowing a known violation. Use Dismiss to hide a recommendation you are not addressing in this project. Accepted records include reviewer name, date, and engineering note.
