Dissimilar Metals Corrosion Reference

Method and Engineering Context

This reference tool estimates galvanic corrosion severity when two different metals are electrically connected in an electrolyte. It is designed for early-stage engineering checks (for example, aluminum cable tray with stainless steel hardware). Outputs are planning-level estimates, not a substitute for project corrosion specifications.

Driving potential = E(cathodic metal) - E(anodic metal)
Rate ∝ Driving potential × environment severity × area ratio × temperature × isolation quality
